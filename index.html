About

I am a Professor in Civil Engineering at Tianjin University. My research focuses on human-building interaction, infrastructure resilience, and AI-driven modeling of the built environment. I develop computational, immersive, and data-driven methods, including digital twins, virtual reality, large language model agents, and multimodal sensing, to support disaster response, safety, and intelligent decision-making.

  • Mar 2026 I am excited to share that I am joining Tianjin University as a full professor! I am actively recruiting students to join my research group.

Selected Project

VR Training for Active Shooter Preparedness

A VR training platform that places healthcare workers inside realistic active-shooter scenarios in a virtual hospital environment. The project uses interactive simulation to study behavior, strengthen preparedness, and provide feedback for safer emergency decision-making.
